HTML Element Definitions

<h1>
A main heading.
<h2>
A level 2 heading.
<h3>
A level 3 heading.
<h4>
A level 4 heading.
<h5>
A level 5 heading.
<h6>
The level 6 heading.
<p>
A paragraph.
<ul>
A bulleted list.
<ol>
A numbered list.
<li>
A list item.
<strong>
Indicates strong importance.
<em>
Indicates emphasis.
<blockquote>
Indicates a long quotation that is a paragraph.
<q>
Indicates a short quotation within a paragraph.
<strong>
Indicates strong importance.
<abbr>
Indicates an abbreviation or acronym.
<cite>
Indicates a reference to a piece of work.
<dfn>
Indicates the defining instance of a new term.
<address>
Indicates contact details for the author of the page.
<ins>
Indicates that content has been inserted.
<del>
Indicates that content has been deleted.
<s>
Indicates the content is no longer accurate.
